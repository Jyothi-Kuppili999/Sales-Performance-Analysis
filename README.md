# Sales Performance Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project analyzes sales performance using SQL and Power BI to uncover business insights related to revenue trends, customer behavior, regional performance, and product performance.

The goal of this project is to transform raw sales data into actionable business insights through data cleaning, SQL analysis, KPI development, and interactive Power BI dashboards.

---

## Business Questions

* Which regions generate the highest revenue?
* Which customer segments contribute most to sales?
* How has revenue changed over time?
* Which customers drive the largest share of revenue?
* Which categories and products perform best?
* Is revenue concentrated among a few customers or distributed across many?

---

## Tools & Technologies

* SQL (MySQL)
* Power BI
* DAX
* Excel

---

## Dataset

Superstore Sales Dataset containing approximately 9,800 sales transactions across multiple regions, customer segments, and product categories.

---

## SQL Analysis

Performed data cleaning and business analysis using SQL:

* Data profiling and validation
* Date conversion and standardization
* Duplicate checks
* Revenue analysis
* Regional analysis
* Customer analysis
* Product analysis
* Year-over-Year growth analysis using window functions (LAG)

---

## Power BI Dashboard Pages

### 1. Executive Dashboard

Provides an overall business performance overview including:

* Total Revenue
* Total Orders
* Total Customers
* Revenue Growth %
* Top Region
* Revenue Trend
* Revenue by Region
* Revenue by Category
* Revenue by Segment

### 2. Regional Analysis

Provides detailed regional insights including:

* Revenue by State
* Customer Distribution
* Revenue by Category
* Revenue by Segment

### 3. Customer Analysis

Analyzes customer behavior through:

* Top Customers by Revenue
* Customer Contribution %
* Average Revenue per Customer
* Segment Analysis

### 4. Product Analysis

Evaluates product performance through:

* Revenue by Category
* Revenue by Sub-Category
* Top Products by Revenue
* Product KPIs

---

## Key Insights

* West region generated the highest revenue.
* Technology was the highest revenue-generating category.
* Consumer segment contributed the largest share of revenue.
* Revenue showed strong growth in 2017 and 2018.
* Revenue was distributed across customers, reducing dependency on a small customer base.
